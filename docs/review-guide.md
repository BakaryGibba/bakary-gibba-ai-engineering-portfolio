# Review Guide

## For Recruiters

Start with the README, then open the Repository Map. Review Compass or Ava AI first for the clearest production AI signal.

## For AI Engineers

Focus on:

- architecture diagrams;
- AI workflow sections;
- data and retrieval designs;
- engineering decisions;
- challenges and trade-offs.

## For Graduate Supervisors

Focus on:

- research direction;
- explainability and AI safety themes;
- LLM systems and evaluation;
- multi-agent and RAG interests;
- how production experience informs research questions.

## For Technical Interviewers

Use the featured case studies to discuss:

- system boundaries;
- failure modes;
- trade-offs;
- data modeling;
- AI evaluation;
- migration from prototype to production.
