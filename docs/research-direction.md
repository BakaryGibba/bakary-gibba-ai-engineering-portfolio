## Research Thesis Direction

The current portfolio suggests a research direction around reliable, explainable, human-centered AI systems. The strongest connection between the engineering work and future research is the question:

> How can AI systems provide useful assistance while remaining grounded, auditable, and understandable to human decision-makers?

## Graduate Study Alignment

The portfolio is relevant for MSc or PhD supervisors interested in:

- applied machine learning systems;
- LLM-based assistants;
- explainable AI in practical interfaces;
- agentic workflow evaluation;
- AI governance and safety in production contexts.
